01 · Overview

Privacy at a glance

WiFi Connect ("the App") is a local Wi-Fi credential manager. It stores network names and passwords exclusively on your device and makes no network connections of any kind. This policy explains how data is handled and confirms what is not collected.

This policy applies to all versions of the App distributed as an Android APK by MiRo Digital Tools, created by Mark Eumir Turingan.

✓ The App collects no personal data, makes no network requests, contains no ads, and stores all information exclusively on your device. App Lock authentication (Pro feature) uses your device PIN and is handled entirely by the device OS — no PIN or credential data is accessed or stored by the App.

02 · Data Collected

What we collect

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information. The only data it stores is what you explicitly enter or scan — and it stays on your device.

Data typeWhat it containsWhere it livesSent externally?
Saved Wi-Fi networksNetwork names (SSIDs), passwords, security type, and any notes you addDevice local storageNever
Scanned QR dataCredentials decoded from a Wi-Fi QR code scan — processed on-device, then stored locally if you choose to saveDevice local storageNever
Imported QR stringsCredentials decoded from a pasted QR string — processed on-device onlyDevice local storageNever

No usage analytics, crash reports, device identifiers, advertising IDs, or any other telemetry are collected.

03 · Storage

How your data is stored

All saved Wi-Fi credentials are stored in the App's sandboxed local storage on your device. This data is only accessible to the App and is not shared with other installed applications.

  • Saved networks persist between sessions until you delete individual entries or uninstall the App.
  • Deleting an entry through the App's interface permanently removes it — there is no undo.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all saved data permanently. There is currently no export or backup function.
Note: MiRo Digital Tools has no access to your device and cannot view, retrieve, or recover any Wi-Fi credentials stored in the App.
04 · Permissions

App permissions

The App requests only the permissions required for its stated features. No permission is used beyond its stated purpose.

PermissionWhy it is needed
CameraRequired only when you tap Scan QR Code. The camera is used exclusively to read Wi-Fi QR codes. No photos are taken, stored, or transmitted.
Device PIN / Screen LockRequired only when App Lock is enabled (Pro feature). Used to authenticate the user before opening the app, triggering an export, or copying credentials. Authentication is handled entirely by the device operating system — the App does not access, store, or transmit your PIN.

The App does not request internet access, storage, microphone, contacts, location, call logs, or any other sensitive permissions. The camera permission is requested only when actively scanning a QR code. The device PIN/screen lock permission is requested only when App Lock is enabled by the user (Pro feature).

No internet permission. WiFi Connect cannot send data over the internet — it has no internet permission in its manifest.
05 · Third-Party Services

Third-party services

The App contains no third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics libraries, crash reporting tools, social media integrations, or tracking frameworks of any kind.

  • No advertising networks or ad SDKs.
  • No analytics or crash reporting (e.g. Firebase, Crashlytics, Sentry).
  • No social login or identity providers.
  • No cloud storage or sync services.
  • No network requests are ever made by the App.
06 · Children's Privacy

Children's privacy

The App is a general-purpose utility tool. It does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Because the App collects no personal information from any user and makes no network connections, it presents no data risk to users of any age.
